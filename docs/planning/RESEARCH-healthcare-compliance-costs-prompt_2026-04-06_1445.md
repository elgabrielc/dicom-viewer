### Which Health Systems Require HITRUST?

**Require HITRUST certification from vendors:**
- CVS Caremark
- Health Care Services Corp.
- Highmark
- Humana
- UnitedHealthcare Group
- Anthem

**HITRUST adoption:**
- 81% of hospitals have adopted or are adopting HITRUST CSF
- 80% of health plans have adopted or are adopting HITRUST CSF
- Epic and Kaiser are HITRUST Alliance board members

**SOC 2 alone may be accepted by:**
- Smaller health systems without formal vendor security programs
- Tech-forward health systems that accept SOC 2 + HIPAA attestation
- Organizations early in their third-party risk management maturity

**Practical guidance:** Most enterprise healthcare customers expect HITRUST certification or a clear remediation path. Start with SOC 2, then pursue HITRUST. Combined audits save 20-30% vs. doing them separately.

### Monthly Cost Estimates by Study Volume

Assumptions: CT study = 200 MB, MRI study = 100 MB. Mixed workload assumed 50/50 CT/MRI = 150 MB avg per study. Using "warm" storage tier pricing. API costs minimal at low volumes.

#### AWS HealthImaging

| Studies/mo | Data (GB) | Frequent Access (first 30d) | After 30d Archive | Monthly (steady state) |
|------------|-----------|----------------------------|--------------------|----------------------|
| 100 | 15 | $1.58 | $0.09 | ~$2 |
| 1,000 | 150 | $15.75 | $0.90 | ~$17 |
| 10,000 | 1,500 | $157.50 | $9.00 | ~$167 |

At scale: 10K studies/mo growing 12 months = ~18 TB stored. Steady-state cost after tiering: **~$108/mo** for archive + ~$158/mo for current month's ingest = **~$266/mo**.

**Key insight:** AWS HealthImaging is remarkably cheap at the archive tier. A startup storing 10K studies/month for a year pays roughly $100-$300/mo total, not thousands.

#### Google Cloud Healthcare API

## 4. Ambra Health / Medical Image Exchange

### Ambra Health (now Intelerad)

Ambra Health was acquired by Intelerad in 2021, and subsequently acquired Life Image in 2022, creating the largest medical image exchange network in the world (80+ billion images).

**Pricing (not publicly listed, estimates from secondary sources):**

| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Per-study fee | $5-$7/study (smaller practices) |
| Monthly minimum | ~$500/mo for 1-3 users |

| Implementation | $2,000-$5,000 (setup, training, data migration) |
| Enterprise pricing | Custom, volume discounts at scale |

**Pricing is not public.** Ambra uses customized pricing tailored to each organization. The per-study model means costs scale linearly with volume -- a significant consideration for a startup planning to grow.

### Alternative Networks

| Vendor | Notes |
|--------|-------|

## Key Takeaways

1. **SOC 2 is the entry ticket.** Budget $50K-$120K Year 1, $30K-$50K ongoing. Non-negotiable for enterprise healthcare sales.

2. **HITRUST is expensive but often required.** The big payers (UHC, Humana, Anthem, CVS) require it. Budget $35K (e1) to $100K+ (r2) Year 1 on top of SOC 2.

3. **Cloud DICOM storage is cheap.** $15-$167/mo for 10K studies. Storage is not the cost problem.

4. **Ambra per-study fees are the silent killer.** At $5-$7/study, a startup doing 10K studies/mo pays $600K-$840K/yr just in exchange fees. Evaluate DICOMweb direct integration before committing to Ambra.

5. **Epic integration is a capital investment.** ~$300K in engineering + $35K in Epic fees + ongoing $60K-$90K/yr maintenance. 6-18 month timeline. Middleware (Redox at $45K/yr) can reduce per-customer deployment cost.

6. **Legal costs are front-loaded.** $10K-$30K Year 1, $3K-$8K ongoing. Template reuse reduces future costs.

7. **The compliance platform market is mature and competitive.** Vanta, Drata, Secureframe, Sprinto all in the $8K-$25K/yr range. Negotiate hard -- 15-30% discounts are standard.

8. **Start with SOC 2, not HITRUST.** SOC 2 opens most doors. Add HITRUST when enterprise payer customers require it.
