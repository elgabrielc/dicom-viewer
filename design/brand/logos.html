Logo Explorations

Based on the hand sketches: a horizontal path that diverges, an upward arrow suggesting better outcomes, and a pulse/heartbeat element.

A. Path Diverge

Direct translation of sketch 2. A horizontal path that continues straight while a second path breaks upward. The expected vs. the different.

B. Ascending Break

Simplified: a single path that changes direction upward. No "expected" path shown — just the break toward something better. Minimal, bold.

C. Pulse Diverge

From sketch 1. A heartbeat pulse that reaches a divergence point — one path continues flat, the other breaks upward. Health signal meets different outcome.

D. Growth Arrow

The simplest read from sketch 1. A single bold arrow rising upward. Pure trajectory — no complexity, just direction. Works as a favicon and app icon.

E. Arrow Fork

From sketch 3. A vertical stem splits into two arrowed paths. Closest to the classic "divergent" symbol. Both paths lead somewhere — they just differ.

F. Track Diverge

Double rail from sketch 2. Two parallel lines travel together, then one breaks upward. Reads as "we were on the same track, then we diverged."
