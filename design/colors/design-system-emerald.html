Usage Guidelines

How to apply the palette across the product.

Do
Use warm neutrals (Light, Dawn) as backgrounds. Let the UI breathe. Use amber for primary actions and interactive elements. Use emerald for success states, confirmations, and completed items. Keep the viewer dark — it's a medical imaging standard.
Don't
Don't use amber and emerald as equal-weight surface colors — one is for action, one is for status. Don't use bright white (#FFF) as a background; use Light (#FFF8F3) instead. Don't bring the warm palette into the viewer canvas — dark mode is functional, not aesthetic.
Library & Onboarding
Warm, light, spacious. Dawn backgrounds with white cards. Serif headings for brand moments. Amber buttons for primary CTAs ("Import Study," "Open Viewer"). Emerald badges for modality tags and sync status.
Image Viewer
Dark emerald base for clinical viewing. Replace blue accent (#4A9EFF) with amber (#EC9907) for active tools, selected series, and interactive highlights. Keep text in neutral grays. The transition from warm library to dark viewer should feel like dimming the lights.
