What you get

Add a folder, done

Import your imaging folder and myRadOne reads every file, groups by study and series, and builds your library automatically. No manual sorting. No more running CDs.

Reports

If you have a clinical report in MyChart or another software from your doctor, you might not have the images in the same place. If you picked up a CD of your images from medical records, it probably doesn't have your final report on it. Now you can easily keep the radiologist's words together with the very images they describe.

Comments and notes

This can all get pretty complicated. Write down whatever's on your mind, and check back whenever you like.

View your images

Accurate image viewing and simple tools built-in, so you (or your next doctor) can take a closer look.
